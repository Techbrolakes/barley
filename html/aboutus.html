About Us

Our Mission Statement

We are committed to making BarleyField Nigeria Limited God-Inspired and one stop Company that provides Turnkey solutions and construction related field. Where qualified and experienced professionals are motivated to offer quality services to our customers.

Introduction

Barleyfield Limited is a Civil/Structural Engineering Contractors registered under the Company and Allied Matters Decree of 1990 with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria also a Civil Engineering Company registered under the Registrar General Department, Accra, Ghana.
BARLEYFIELD NIG. LTD. (RC. 447226) BARLEYFIELD GHANA (RC 19689).
We undertake Civil and Structural Engineering works, Building and Building Engineering services, Sub-soil Investigation, Piling, Telecommunication Infrastructure services and Electrical Engineering jobs for various organization and clients. The company is managed by a team of experienced civil/structural engineers, who oversee a multi-disciplinary team of specialists for its various jobs. In conjunction with an efficient management and service support team, we offer reliable and quality services at optimal costs to our clients.

Essentially, we provide an array of services in the following areas:

  • Civil works
  • BTS Site Builds
  • Soil Investigation
  • Piling for deep Foundation
  • Foundation for Tower and Mast
  • Erection of Tower
  • RF works

  • Antenna and Dishes Installation
  • Grounding/Earthen Works
