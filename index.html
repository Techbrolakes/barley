Welcome to BarleyField Ltd.

BarleyField Limited is a Civil/Structural Engineering Contractors registered under the Company and Allied Matters Decree of 1990 with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria also a Civil Engineering Company registered under the Registrar General Department, Accra, Ghana. We undertake civil engineering works, sub-soil investigation, piling, telecommunication infrastructure services and Electrical Engineering jobs for various organization/clients. The company is managed by an experienced Civil engineer, who oversees a multi-disciplinary team of specialists for its various jobs. In conjunction with an efficient management and service support team, we offer reliable and quality services at optimal costs to our clients.

Our Services

  • Civil works
  • BTS Site Builds
  • Soil Investigation
  • Piling for deep Foundation
  • Foundation for Tower and Mast
  • Erection of Tower
  • RF works
  • Equipment Shelter works
  • Antenna and Dishes Installation
  • Grounding/Earthen Works
  • Generator Installation
  • Broadcast
  • Electricity Supply Services
